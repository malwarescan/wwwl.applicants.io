# Risk Scoring Methodology

## Scoring Factors

### Compensation Structure (0-30 points)

- Unpaid training periods: -10 points
- Commission-only positions: -15 points
- Below-market base salary: -5 points
- No benefits provided: -5 points

### Hiring Patterns (0-25 points)

- High turnover indicators: -10 points
- Aggressive recruitment: -8 points
- Vague job descriptions: -7 points

### Business Model (0-25 points)

- MLM/pyramid structure indicators: -15 points
- Door-to-door sales: -10 points
- Questionable business practices: -10 points

### Reported Patterns (0-20 points)

- Consistent negative themes: -10 points
- Legal action history: -10 points
- Regulatory warnings: -10 points

## Calculation

Total risk score = Sum of all factor deductions from 100

Example:
- Compensation: -20
- Hiring: -15
- Business Model: -10
- Reported Patterns: -5
- **Final Score: 50** (Medium Risk)

## Updates

Scores are recalculated when:
- New data becomes available
- Patterns change significantly
- Structural changes occur
