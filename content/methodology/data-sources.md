# Data Sources

## Primary Sources

### Public Job Postings
- Job board listings
- Company career pages
- Recruitment platform data

### Compensation Data
- Salary reporting sites
- Industry benchmarks
- Public disclosures

### Business Records
- Corporate registrations
- Regulatory filings
- Industry associations

## Aggregation Methods

- Pattern identification
- Statistical analysis
- Cross-reference verification

## Update Frequency

- Job postings: Weekly
- Compensation data: Monthly
- Business records: Quarterly
- Risk scores: As data updates

## Data Limitations

- Publicly available information only
- May not reflect recent changes
- Individual experiences vary
- Not comprehensive of all factors
