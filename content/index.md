#title
How It Works

#description
Our process follows five steps:

::steps
  :::step
  ---
  icon: i-lucide-database
  ---
  #title
  Collection

  #description
  Public posts, reviews, and discussions are collected from multiple sources
  :::

  :::step
  ---
  icon: i-lucide-file-check
  ---
  #title
  Normalization

  #description
  Company mentions are normalized into consistent entities
  :::

  :::step
  ---
  icon: i-lucide-search
  ---
  #title
  Pattern Detection

  #description
  Repeated recruiting and compensation patterns are detected
  :::

  :::step
  ---
  icon: i-lucide-bar-chart-3
  ---
  #title
  Risk Scoring

  #description
  A transparent risk score summarizes pattern density and repetition
  :::

  :::step
  ---
  icon: i-lucide-file-text
  ---
  #title
  Publication

  #description
  Results are published as structured company intelligence pages
  :::
::

Each company page summarizes what is publicly reported, how often similar patterns appear, and what candidates commonly experience.

::u-page-section{class="dark:bg-neutral-950"}
#title
Important Clarification

#description
Applicants.io does not claim that any company has engaged in unlawful conduct. All information is aggregated from publicly available sources and summarized to highlight recurring patterns described by multiple independent individuals.

If you represent a company and believe information is inaccurate or incomplete, you may request a review by providing verifiable documentation.
