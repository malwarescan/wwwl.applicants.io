# Page → Search Intent Map

This document maps each page/section to its primary search intent and example queries. Use this as an internal reference and sanity check.

## Intent Mapping

| Page / Section | Primary Intent | Example Queries |
|----------------|----------------|-----------------|
| `/` | Pre-decision evaluation | "is this job legit", "research company before interview" |
| `/companies` | Company lookup | "company reviews", "is [company] legit" |
| `/companies/[slug]` | Decision window | "[company] job review", "[company] pay structure" |
| `/guides/commission-only-jobs` | Pattern explanation | "what is commission only job" |
| `/guides/unpaid-training` | Risk explanation | "unpaid training job legal" |
| `/methodology/risk-scoring` | Trust / transparency | "how does Applicants.io score companies" |
| `/networks/[slug]` | Structural research | "smart circle recruiting", "devilcorp network" |

## Rules

**If a page does not cleanly answer one of these intents, it should not exist.**

Each page must:
- Answer a specific candidate question
- Match a clear search intent
- Provide actionable information
- Use plain language
- Avoid technical jargon

## Intent Categories

### Pre-Decision Evaluation
Pages that help candidates evaluate opportunities before accepting interviews or offers.

### Company Lookup
Pages that help candidates find information about specific companies.

### Decision Window
Pages that provide information during the critical decision-making period (interview → offer → acceptance).

### Pattern Explanation
Pages that explain common recruiting patterns and what they mean.

### Risk Explanation
Pages that explain potential risks and how to identify them.

### Trust / Transparency
Pages that explain how Applicants.io works and builds trust.

### Structural Research
Pages that explain recruiting networks and organizational structures.
